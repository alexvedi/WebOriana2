TERMINAL 1

2024

The Glastonbury festival has a proud history of finding space for the travelling community. After new age travellers were persecuted and attacked in the 1990's it became a place for a marginalised people to meet, trade and forge new alliances. Terminal 1 aims to honour this spirit and create space for a modern day travelling community - migrants.

In a time where the lines drawn on a map by our ancestors have come to define and divide our world, this is a place without borders that celebrates our shared humanity. Hosted by artists, performers and crew from all over the world it is unified by the simple message - no human is illegal.
